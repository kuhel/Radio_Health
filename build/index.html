THE NEW AND ADVANCING UK HEALTH RADIO

We have developed and grown into the UK Health Radio you see today and now we want to thank all of our friends, listeners and healthcare professionals, as without you this would not have been possible.

It has only been two and a half years since UK Health Radio went live. Through the positive response, feedback and support received from around the world, we know we are empowering people to attaining and/or keeping their health freedom.

We found support in the public, a variety of companies and specifically DataArt, who without, UK Health Radio would not be what you see today. We are truly grateful to all.

UK Health Radio & DataArt look forward to celebrating with friends and peers!
